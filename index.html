1. Introduction

The Turf User Dashboard is designed to provide users with a seamless way to book, manage, and keep track of their turf reservations. The dashboard allows users to access available turfs, view upcoming bookings, manage their account settings, and receive notifications. This manual serves as a guide to help users navigate the features of the dashboard effectively.

2. Getting Started

2.1 Logging In

To log in to the Turf User Dashboard:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Enter your registered email address and password.
  3. Click the Login button.
  4. If login is successful, you will be redirected to the dashboard. If not, an error message will appear; check your credentials and try again.

2.2 Logging Out

To log out:

  1. From the dashboard header, click the Logout button.
  2. You will be logged out and redirected to the login page.

3. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is divided into two main areas:

3.1 Sidebar Navigation

The sidebar on the left side of the screen contains links to various sections of the dashboard:

  • My Bookings: View all upcoming and past bookings.
  • Available Turfs: Access a list of available turfs with details.
  • Notifications: See reminders and notifications about bookings.
  • Profile: Update personal details and view membership status.
  • Settings: Adjust account preferences.
  • Logout: Sign out of your account.

3.2 Main Content Area

This area displays the content for the selected sidebar option. By default, it shows a welcome message and highlights essential user information, like upcoming bookings.

4. Features and Functionalities

4.1 Profile Overview

The Profile Overview section shows key information about your account, including:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Membership Status (e.g., Basic, Premium)

To access this section, click Profile in the sidebar.

4.2 Viewing Available Turfs

The Available Turfs section displays all turf options, each with details such as:

  • Turf Name
  • Location
  • Capacity
  • Availability Status

To access, click Available Turfs in the sidebar. Here, you can browse different turfs to decide which one to book.

4.3 Booking a Turf

To book a turf:

  1. Go to the Available Turfs section.
  2. Select the turf you want to book.
  3. Choose a date and time for the booking.
  4. Click the Book Now button.
  5. Confirm your booking details, and then submit.
  6. After confirmation, you will see your booking listed in the My Bookings section.

4.4 Managing Bookings

The My Bookings section shows all your upcoming and past bookings. To access:

  1. Click My Bookings in the sidebar.
  2. You can view, edit, or cancel upcoming bookings as necessary.

To cancel a booking:

  1. Select the booking, click the Cancel button, and confirm.

4.5 Notifications and Reminders

The Notifications section keeps you updated on upcoming bookings, cancellations, and important updates. Notifications will appear with a timestamp for easy tracking.

4.6 Updating Profile Information

To update personal details like your name or email:

  1. Go to Profile in the sidebar.
  2. Click Edit Profile, update the information, and save changes.

5. Settings

5.1 Changing Password

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar.
  2. Select Change Password.
  3. Enter your current password and the new password.
  4. Confirm the new password and click Save.

5.2 Notification Preferences

To customize your notification preferences:

  1. Go to Settings > Notifications.
  2. Choose your notification type and frequency.
  3. Save changes to update your preferences.

6. Troubleshooting and FAQs

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?
A: Click the Forgot Password link on the login page. Enter your registered email to receive a reset link.

Q: How do I view past bookings?
A: In the My Bookings section, scroll to find past bookings listed chronologically.

Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?
A: Check your Notification Preferences in the Settings section to ensure notifications are enabled.

Q: How can I contact support?
A: Use the Contact Support option in the settings or visit the Help section for more assistance.
